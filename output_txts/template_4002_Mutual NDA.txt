Confidential Information

1The parties agree that Confidential Information shall mean any and all information or data of any nature, tangible or intangible, oral or in writing and in any format or medium, which by its nature or content is or ought reasonably to be identifiable as confidential and/or proprietary to the Disclosing Party or which is provided or disclosed in confidence, and which the Disclosing Party or any person acting on behalf of the Disclosing Party may disclose or provide to the Receiving Party or which may come to the knowledge of the Receiving Party by whatsoever means. Without limitation, the Confidential Information of the Disclosing Party shall include the following even if it is not marked as being confidential, restricted or proprietary (or any similar designation): 


1any information relating to property, development proposals, plans, drawings, feasibility studies, intellectual property, products, research and development, market opportunities, customers, statistics, pricing, reports, systems, marketing, methods, promotional and advertising plans and strategies, financial plans and models, employees and office bearers, and business affairs of the Disclosing Party 


2the contractual and financial arrangements between the Disclosing Party and others with whom it has business arrangements of whatsoever nature, including potential customers/clients, suppliers and service providers and business associates of the Disclosing Party whom it has not yet contacted but intends contacting for the purposes of doing business 


3all other matters or information which relate or may relate to the business affairs of the Disclosing Party in respect of which information is not readily available in the ordinary course of business to a competitor of the Disclosing Party 


4details of the financial structure and/or operating results of the Disclosing Party or any of its affiliates 


5details of any shareholding or ownership arrangements in respect of the Disclosing Party 


6the fact of and content of: 


1this Agreement 


2the Purpose and 


3any agreement which may be concluded between the Parties pursuant to the Purpose 


7information which is disclosed in circumstances of confidence or would be understood by the Parties, exercising reasonable business judgment, to be Confidential Information and 


8all other Confidential Information in whatever form, disclosed or communicated to the Receiving Party or acquired by the Receiving Party from the Disclosing Party
